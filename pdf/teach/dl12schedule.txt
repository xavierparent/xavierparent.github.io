DEONTIC LOGIC 2012


Lecturers
--------
D. Gabbay, X. parent, L. van der Torre


Objective
--------
The objective of this course is to introduce students to formal methods for normative reasoning (deontic logic)


Description
---------

The 'Handbook of Deontic Logic' is used as teaching material. One chapter is presented per lecture. 

Schedule
-------

Background:
 -Historical survey
- Imperatives
Traditional developments:
 - permission
- conflicts between norms
- constitutive norms
New standards:
- alternative semantics
- Input-output logic
- Algebraic approach to normative systems

During the last lecture students present their project
